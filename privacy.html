Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2026 • Wolf Creek Voice • Pagosa Springs, Colorado

Wolf Creek Voice ("we", "us", or "our") provides AI-powered voice agent services to small businesses in Southwest Colorado. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. We keep this plain and specific because vague privacy policies serve no one.

Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways — directly from you and automatically through our services.

Directly from you:

  • Name and phone number when you submit a callback request on our website
  • Business name, contact details, and service requirements when you engage us as a client
  • Email address and message content when you contact us directly

Automatically through our services:

  • Voice call audio and transcripts processed through our AI voice platform (Retell AI)
  • Call metadata including duration, timestamp, and caller-provided information
  • Basic website usage data (page visits, browser type) via standard web analytics

How We Use Your Information

  • To return your callback request and connect you with our AI voice agent demo
  • To deliver voice agent services to our business clients on your behalf
  • To improve agent performance through call review and transcript analysis
  • To communicate with you about your inquiry or service relationship
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

Voice Calls and Recordings

When you interact with a Wolf Creek Voice AI agent — whether via our website demo or as a caller to a client's business line — your call may be recorded and transcribed. This is used to:

  • Fulfill the purpose of the call (booking, lead capture, information routing)
  • Review call quality and improve agent accuracy
  • Provide call logs and transcripts to the business client whose line you called

By engaging with an AI voice agent on our platform, you consent to the call being recorded and processed by AI systems. If you do not consent, you may end the call at any time.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party providers to deliver our services. Each is contractually bound to handle your data responsibly:

  • Retell AI — our voice AI platform. Processes call audio, generates transcripts, and powers our agents. Retell is SOC 2 Type II certified and HIPAA compliant. See retellai.com/privacy.
  • Twilio — telephony provider for our 970 phone number. Handles call routing and connectivity.
  • GoHighLevel (GHL) — CRM platform used to store lead information captured during calls.

Data Retention

  • Website callback requests: retained for 90 days or until actioned
  • Call recordings and transcripts: retained for up to 12 months for quality review, then deleted
  • Client business data: retained for the duration of the service relationship plus 12 months
  • You may request earlier deletion at any time (see Your Rights below)

Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of any non-essential communications

Colorado residents have additional rights under the Colorado Privacy Act (CPA), including the right to opt out of certain data processing and to appeal our decisions about your data. European residents have rights under GDPR including data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission, access controls, and working only with vendors who maintain rigorous security certifications. No system is perfectly secure — if you believe your data has been compromised, contact us immediately.

Children

Our services are not directed at anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated directly to active clients.
